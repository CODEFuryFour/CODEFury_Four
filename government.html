Government Schemes and Subsidies

The government offers various schemes and subsidies to support farmers and promote agricultural development. Here are some of the key schemes and subsidies available:

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

PM-KISAN is a central sector scheme that provides financial assistance to landholding farmers. Under this scheme, farmers receive Rs. 6000 per year in three equal installments through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) mode.

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

PMFBY offers affordable crop insurance to farmers, covering risks from pre-sowing to post-harvest. The scheme provides comprehensive risk cover and ensures adequate claim amounts.

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY)

PMKSY aims to enhance water use efficiency in agriculture by promoting micro-irrigation and ensuring water access to every farm.

Kisan Credit Card (KCC) Scheme

The KCC scheme provides farmers with timely access to credit for their agricultural needs, including crop production, maintenance, and other expenses.

Soil Health Card Scheme

This scheme aims to promote soil testing and provide farmers with soil health cards that contain nutrient status and recommendations for improving soil health.

National Agriculture Market (e-NAM)

e-NAM is an online trading platform that connects farmers with buyers, ensuring better price discovery and transparency in agricultural markets.

Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY)

RKVY aims to promote holistic development in agriculture and allied sectors by providing financial assistance to states for implementing agricultural projects.
