About Microinsurance

Microinsurance provides coverage tailored to the needs of low-income individuals and small-scale businesses. It is designed to be affordable and accessible while providing essential protection against risks.

Benefits

  • Affordable premiums.
  • Coverage for health, property, and livelihood risks.
  • Flexible plans suited for small-scale needs.
  • Quick and easy claim process.
