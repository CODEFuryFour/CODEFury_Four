About Micro Loans

Micro loans are small-scale loans provided to individuals and small businesses to support their growth and financial stability. These loans typically have flexible terms and minimal requirements.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be 18 years or older.
  • Should have a valid identity document.
  • Demonstration of income or business feasibility.
  • Minimal credit history requirements.
